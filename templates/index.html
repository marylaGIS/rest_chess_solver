It's a simple app to support chess game

List of available chess figures:

  • king ♔
  • queen ♕
  • rook ♖
  • bishop ♗
  • knight ♘
  • pawn ♙

To check available fields to move for the selected figure type in your browser:

http://127.0.0.1:5000//api/v1/{chess-figure}/{current-field}

for example: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/king/a1
